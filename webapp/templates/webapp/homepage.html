Who are we?

Je Ne Sais Quoi Events is an event planning service specializing in creating an experience in every celebration. Having attended many events over the years and realizing that each event, while promising fun and excitement, always left me with that "something's missing, I just can't put my finger on it" feeling. Well Je Ne Sais Quoi is just that thing! More than fun and excitement, we want every memory of your celebration to be as if the event itself were being relived. Customization, Affordability, and Perfect Execution is our goal and promise to every client. Contact us today for your "Je Ne Sais Quoi" experience!
